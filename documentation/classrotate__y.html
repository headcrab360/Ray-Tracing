Detailed Description

y-rotation of translate instance.

for x: y'= cos(theta)*y+sin(theta)*z and z'= −sin(theta)*y+cos(theta)*z for y: x'= cos(theta)*x+sin(theta)*z and z'= −sin(theta)*x+cos(theta)*z for z: z'= cos(theta)*x+sin(theta)*y and y'= −sin(theta)*x+cos(theta)*y
