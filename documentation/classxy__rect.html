Detailed Description

Rectangle class.

To determine whether a ray hits such a rectangle, we first determine where the ray hits the plane. Recall that a ray P(t)=A+tb has its z component defined by P_z(t)=A_z+tb_z. Rearranging those terms we can solve for what the t is where z=k: t = (kâˆ’A_z)/b_z. Once we have t, we can plug that into the equations for x and y: x=A_x+tb_x and y=A_y+tb_y. It is a hit if x_0<x<x_1 and y_0<y<y_1.
